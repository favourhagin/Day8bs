ABOUT US

Digerati Community is a fast-growing society and community of digerats cooperating together to achieve one objective: to elevate the digital discourse and unleash digital potentials worldwide. We are Digerati, a community empowering students across various secondary schools. Our clubs are established in institutions such as Agbenu Bridge College, University of Agriculture (Jostum), and Corona Private School. Our goal is to unlock digital potential in students, fostering innovation and growth. By providing a platform for digital exploration, we aim to equip students with essential skills. Our community is expanding, with new clubs soon to be established in additional schools. We believe in nurturing the next generation of digital leaders. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and empower students in the digital age. Through our programs, students will gain hands-on experience and valuable insights. We are committed to making a positive impact on the digital landscape. By joining Digerati, students can unlock their full potential and thrive in a rapidly evolving world.
